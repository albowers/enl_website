Participate in Our Research

We are currently recruiting participants for several studies related to speech motor control and neurofeedback for stuttering.

Current Studies:

  • Neurofeedback training for adults who stutter (ages 18-45) - This study involves 12 sessions of EEG neurofeedback training aimed at improving speech fluency.
  • Beta burst dynamics during speech tasks (ages 18-35) - A single-session study examining brain activity during various speech production tasks.
  • EEG during speech perception and production - Seeking both fluent speakers and people who stutter, ages 18-60.

Participants may be compensated for their time and travel expenses. For more information about participation eligibility and scheduling, please contact us at eeglab@uark.edu or call (479) 575-4509.
